Time

In the real world, time is continuous. But here, time is divided into discrete units called frames. The framerate (in frames per second) determines how fast the animation will run.

Try slowing the framerate (e.g. to 10) by adjusting the slider or the number panel. Then click on the cell again and watch it undergo an action potential.

You can also stop and restart the animation with the play/pause button. When paused, you can advance the animation one frame at a time by clicking on the advance frame button

Try the following:

  1. Pause the animation
  2. Click on the cell again to depolarise it
  3. Advance the animation frame by frame
